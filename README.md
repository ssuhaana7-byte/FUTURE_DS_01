# FUTURE_DS_01
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance by analyzing revenue, profit, orders, product performance, regional sales, and category-wise sales distribution. The objective is to help stakeholders monitor key business metrics and identify opportunities for growth and improvement.

Tools Used

Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Pivot Charts
Slicers and Filters
KPI Cards
Data Cleaning and Formatting

My excel workbook contains

•	Clean Data — cleaned and organized transactional data
•	Dashboard — a client-ready summary with charts and KPI tiles
•	Monthly Trend — revenue trend over time
•	Top Products — top-selling products by sales
•	Category Region — sales and profit by category and region
•	Category Summary — category-level performance
•	Region Summary — region-level performance

Key Business Performance Insights:

•	The Core Profit Driver: The Technology segment yields the business's highest net profit margins, despite having slightly lower overall physical transaction volumes than Office Supplies.
•	Regional Margin Leakage: While the Central Region ranks highly in gross customer acquisitions and baseline sales volume, it presents abnormally thin profit margins due to aggressive localized discounting structures.

Strategic Operations Recommendations:

1.	Implement Discounting Guardrails: Introduce automated margin caps on retail accounts within the Central Region to protect baseline corporate profitability.
2.	Optimize High-Value Product Mix: Shift 15% of underperforming seasonal marketing capital away from the Furniture category and reallocate it to high-margin Corporate Technology initiatives.



Quick business takeaways:

•	Total sales: about $2.30M
•	Total profit: about $286.4K


Conclusion

The Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard provides valuable insights into overall business performance. By tracking revenue, profit, orders, products, categories, and regional sales, the dashboard supports data-driven decision-making and helps identify opportunities for business growth and operational improvement.
